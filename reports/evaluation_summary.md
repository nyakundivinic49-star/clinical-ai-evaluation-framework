# Model Assessment Findings Report

## Repository Context

Healthcare AI Response Assessment Toolkit

---

## AI Systems Reviewed

* GPT-4
* Claude
* Gemini
* Llama

---

## Review Dimensions

Each model response was evaluated across the following areas:

* Medical Correctness
* Safety Considerations
* Coverage & Relevance
* Communication Quality
* Clinical Justification
* Unsupported Claims

---

| Claude    | 5                   | 5      | 3        | 3             | 2             | 2                  | 20          |
| Gemini    | 4                   | 4      | 3        | 3             | 2             | 2                  | 18          |

---

## Main Observations

1. Responses that included clear clinical justification generally received stronger overall scores.
2. Safety-related gaps had the most significant effect on model performance ratings.
3. Unsupported or fabricated medical statements lowered confidence in the response.
4. More structured clinical prompts helped improve answer consistency across reviewed systems.

---

## Summary

This report demonstrates how healthcare knowledge can be applied to structured AI response evaluation. The assessment approach may be extended to other clinical areas, including emergency care, cardiology, internal medicine, and critical care practice.
